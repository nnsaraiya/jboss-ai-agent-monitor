Executive Summary
JBoss AI Monitor — Autonomous Incident Detection & Documentation
Reducing MTTR and operational cost with an AI-powered monitoring agent on Red Hat OpenShift AI
The Challenge

JBoss/WildFly incidents on OpenShift are detected late, analysed manually by senior engineers, and documented inconsistently — or not at all. Each incident consumes 2–3 hours of skilled engineering time and carries the risk of overnight outages going undetected until business hours.

The Solution

The JBoss AI Monitor is an autonomous agent that continuously watches JBoss pods on OpenShift, detects failures in real time, submits the issue context to a Red Hat OpenShift AI–hosted LLM, and creates a structured JIRA ticket with root-cause analysis, resolution steps, and prevention tips — all within 60 seconds of detection, 24 hours a day.
